# Skill: Generate CEO Briefing

Generate the Monday Morning CEO Briefing by auditing the vault's activity logs and task state.

## Instructions

1. **Read** `/Logs/` for all JSON log files from the past 7 days.
2. **Read** `/Done/` to list completed tasks this week.
3. **Read** `/Accounting/Current_Month.md` for financial summary.
4. **Read** `Business_Goals.md` for targets and KPIs.
5. **Read** `Dashboard.md` for current queue status.

6. **Generate** a briefing file at `/Briefings/CEO_Briefing_YYYY-MM-DD.md` with this structure:

```markdown
# CEO Briefing — Week of YYYY-MM-DD

**Generated by:** AI Employee
**Period:** YYYY-MM-DD to YYYY-MM-DD

---

## Executive Summary
<2-3 sentence overview of the week>

---

## Revenue
| | This Week | MTD | Target |
|--|-----------|-----|--------|
| Revenue | $X | $X | $X |
| Trend | ↑/↓ | — | — |

---

## Completed This Week
- [ ] Task 1
- [ ] Task 2

---

## Bottlenecks
| Task | Expected | Actual | Delay | Reason |
|------|----------|--------|-------|--------|

---

## Proactive Suggestions
1. <cost optimization or risk>
2. <upcoming deadline>
3. <automation opportunity>

---
*Generated automatically. Review and archive or act on suggestions.*
```

7. Update `Dashboard.md` with a link to the new briefing.
8. Output: `<promise>TASK_COMPLETE</promise>`

## Usage

```
/generate-briefing
```
